Why coming to N°5 Inn

Tasteful service

Alison is your host and will make sure your visit is the best it can be whether it be for a drink in the bar, food and drink in the cosy snug, great food in the restaurant or just chilling in our new beer garden.

Local flavours

The kitchen is overseen by Chef David who works closely with local suppliers to bring you the best in pub favourites (have a look at the restaurant page for some tasty pics).

Live traditional performances

We also host a night of traditional music in the bar on the 1st Tuesday of each month. Why not pop along for some great foot tapping tunes.
